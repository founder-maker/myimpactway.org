Healing Farm & Early Childhood Rescue (Kenya)

The Impactway Children's Initiative Healing Farm is a specialized rescue center for Kenya’s most vulnerable street children, ages **4–10**. We replace fear and instability with trust, routine, and unconditional care.

Impact: Restoring Emotional Security

Our trauma-informed program is designed for deep psychological healing, giving children the cognitive scaffolding necessary to thrive.

  • **Nature-Based Recovery:** Children exposed to natural routines, farm care, and predictable rhythms show reduced hypervigilance.
  • **Cognitive Readiness:** Early literacy and cooperative play prepare children for transition to primary school.
  • **Verifiable Outcome (M&E):** We track Growth (weight/height), Emotional Regulation, and Attachment Behaviors.

CHILD SAFEGUARDING MANDATE (Non-Negotiable)

This is a **closed-access therapeutic program**. We strictly prohibit all public visits, ad-hoc volunteers, and guest access to protect the long-term well-being and privacy of the children in our care. **Staff are locally hired and fully vetted.**
