The Iman Children's Healing Farm

Restoring hope and health through holistic, trauma-informed care for orphaned and vulnerable children in Kenya.

Our Model: Healing from the Inside Out

Trauma-Informed Focus

Care is provided by trained professionals focusing on safety, trust, and resilience building, not just symptom management.

Nutritional Healing

Farm-based therapy provides organic, nutrient-dense food, addressing the physical and developmental impacts of prolonged trauma.

Secure Funding

The Farm is 100% funded by profits from the Iman Wellness Center LLC, ensuring permanent operational stability and ethical child protection.
