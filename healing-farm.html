Healing Farm & Early Childhood Rescue (Kenya)

Impactway’s Healing Farm is a specialized rescue, healing, and early development center for Kenya’s most vulnerable street children, ages 4–10. We replace fear and instability with trust, routine, and unconditional care.

Impact: Restoring Emotional Security and Stability

Our trauma-informed program is designed for deep psychological healing, giving children the cognitive scaffolding necessary to thrive. We focus on nature-based recovery to improve emotional regulation and attachment.

  • **Nature-Based Recovery:** Children exposed to natural routines, farm care, and predictable rhythms show reduced hypervigilance and improved emotional regulation.
  • **Cognitive Readiness:** Early literacy, cooperative play, and emotional literacy prepare children for successful transition to primary school.
  • **Verifiable Outcome (M&E):** We track **Growth (weight/height)**, **Emotional Regulation Markers**, and **Attachment Behaviors** (assessed by a registered therapist) to confirm stabilization.

Program Model: The 12-Month Path to Healing

1. Rescue & Stabilization

Immediate objectives: **Safety**, **Nourishment**, **Regulation**. Activities include emergency medical care, establishing a predictable daily routine, and sensory play to reduce fear.

2. Trauma-Informed Care

Focus: **Emotional Stabilization**. Core activities like gardening, animal interaction, and therapeutic play to build trust and foster secure attachments.

3. Early Learning & Development

Focus: **Cognitive Growth**. Includes early literacy, art therapy, and social skills development, preparing the child for a successful transition to formal education.

4. Family Reintegration

Ongoing case management focused on safe and supported reunification with biological or vetted foster families for long-term placement.

Sustainability Strategy: The Wellness Retreat Model

Our program is built for long-term financial independence. The 7-acre site is divided into three zones: the secure Children's Healing Farm, the Agriculture Zone, and the future Adult Healing Retreat (Year 3).
