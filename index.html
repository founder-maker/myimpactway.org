Our Two Pillars of Sustainable Impact

The Healing Farm & Early Childhood Rescue (Kenya)

We provide stable, trauma-informed residential care for the youngest survivors (ages 4–10). Our focus is on psychological healing and stabilization in a safe, closed-access environment.

$3,827 sponsors one child for a full year of healing.

Digital Literacy Hub (Indonesia)

We empower orphaned adolescents (ages 13–17) with job-ready technology skills. This program bridges the economic gap, moving youth from dependency to employment.

$3,075 funds a full 12-week training cycle for 45 youth.
