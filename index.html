Our Core Initiatives

The Healing Farm & Rescue Program (Kenya)

We provide immediate safety, medical care, and trauma-informed counseling for orphaned and abandoned street children aged 0–10. This farm-based environment uses animal interaction and routine-based attachment to restore security, stability, and trust.

Goal: Rescue and stabilize 20 children in Year 1.

Orphan Digital Literacy Program (Lombok, Indonesia)

We deliver essential digital skills to orphaned youth (ages 13–17) using a mobile 15-laptop learning lab. Through four 12-week cycles annually, we ensure equal, uninterrupted access to skills needed for employment and safe online participation.

Impact: Training 180 orphaned youth every year.
