Our High-Impact Programs

Every dollar supports a specific pathway to healing and independence.

1. Healing Farm & Early Childhood Rescue (Kenya)

A specialized 12-month trauma-informed program providing rescue, stabilization, and early education for vulnerable street children ages 0–10.

  • **Focus:** Emotional Healing & Cognitive Development.
  • **Budget:** $76,100 per year.

2. Orphan Digital Literacy Program (Indonesia)

Equipping orphaned youth (ages 13–17) with job-ready digital skills using a mobile 15-laptop lab to ensure economic independence.

  • **Focus:** Employability & Digital Equity (180 youth trained annually).
  • **Budget:** $12,300 (Year 1 setup).
