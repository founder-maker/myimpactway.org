Our Programs: Empowering Futures, Restoring Hope

ImpactWay runs two primary programs, each secured by guaranteed funding to maximize sustainability and impact.

Digital Literacy Program (Global Focus)

Our Digital Literacy program delivers culturally-aligned tech education to youth in underserved communities globally, starting with a pilot in Lombok, Indonesia. This program is fully funded by profits from the ImpactWay Platform LLC, ensuring long-term program health.

Focus Areas: Basic computer skills, internet safety, coding fundamentals, and pathways to remote work and online entrepreneurship.

Explore the Curriculum

Iman Children's Healing Farm (Kenya Focus)

The Healing Farm provides a safe, nurturing, and trauma-informed environment for orphaned and vulnerable children (ages 4–10) in Kenya. Our model prioritizes nutritional healing, farm therapy, and family-style care leading to reunification.

This program is fully funded by profits from the Iman Wellness Center LLC, isolating it from charity reliance and ensuring ethical, consistent care.
