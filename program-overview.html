Our Global Impact Programs

We focus our efforts on two high-impact initiatives designed to create stable, educated, and empowered futures for vulnerable children and youth worldwide. Click on a program below to see the full operational details, budget, and impact metrics.

Healing Farm & Early Childhood Rescue (Kenya)

A 12-month trauma-informed program for orphaned street children ages **4–10**, providing rescue, medical care, and a stable farm environment for early development.

Focus: Stabilization, Trust, and Early Learning.

Orphan Digital Literacy Program (Indonesia)

Equipping orphaned youth (ages 13–17) with job-ready digital skills using a mobile 15-laptop learning lab, ensuring access to modern economic opportunity.

Focus: Employability, Digital Equity, and Future Skills.
