Our Global Impact Programs

Healing Farm & Early Childhood Rescue (Kenya)

Every child deserves a safe place to heal. Impactway’s Healing Farm is a specialized rescue, healing, and early development center for Kenya’s most vulnerable street children, ages 0–10.

Core Program Phases (12 Months)

  • **Phase 1: Intake & Stabilization (Weeks 1–4):** Immediate objectives include safety, nourishment, and medical care. Activities focus on establishing a predictable routine and gentle introduction to trauma-informed caregivers.
  • **Phase 2: Healing Through Trust & Routine (Months 2–3):** Focus on emotional stabilization through core activities like daily animal interaction, simple gardening tasks, and music and movement therapy.
  • **Phase 3: Development & Early Learning (Months 4–12):** Focus shifts to strengthening cognitive and social development using letters, numbers, storytelling, and cooperative group play.

Monitoring & Evaluation

Key indicators tracked for donor transparency include weight gain, sleep quality, improved emotional regulation, and attachment to caregivers.

Orphan Digital Literacy Program (Lombok, Indonesia)

This program provides vulnerable orphaned adolescents (ages 13–17) with the essential digital skills needed for education, employment, and economic opportunity. We utilize a mobile 15-laptop lab to bring training directly into orphanages.

Program Structure & Impact

  • **Mobile Lab:** A dedicated unit of 15 mid-range laptops, charging cases, and a local instructor.
  • **Annual Reach:** 180 orphaned youth trained per year through four 12-week cycles.
  • **Gender Equity:** Each 45-student cycle includes two classes for girls and one class for boys to ensure gender-balanced access.

Curriculum Modules

  1. Digital Foundations (Typing, file management, internet basics).
  2. Productivity & Communication (Google Docs, email etiquette).
  3. Creative Skills (Canva basics, resume creation).
  4. Digital Citizenship (Online safety, avoiding scams).
