Your Support Makes Healing and Opportunity Possible

Impactway relies on accountable, specific sponsorships. We have broken down our needs into high-impact units so you know exactly where your donation is going. Every dollar funds a child's direct pathway to stability and independence.

Sponsorship Opportunities by Program

1. Healing Farm: Sponsor a Child's Full Year of Healing

This covers the entire 12-month stabilization and trauma-recovery program for one child (ages 0-10) in Kenya.

  • **Cost Per Child (1 Year):** **$3,805**
  • *Includes:* Rescue, medical clearance, trauma counseling, food, clothing, and primary caregiver support.

See Full Program Budget Details → Sponsor a Child ($3,805)

2. Digital Literacy: Sponsor a Full Training Cycle

Fund one full 12-week training cycle, providing 45 orphaned youth with essential job-ready digital skills in Lombok, Indonesia.

  • **Cost Per Student (Per Cycle):** **~$42 USD**
  • **Cost Per 12-Week Cycle (45 Students):** **$3,075**

See Full Program Budget Details → Sponsor a Cycle ($3,075)

3. Technology Investment: Fund a Mobile Laptop Unit

Sponsor the dedicated 15-laptop mobile learning unit that serves 180 youth annually across four cycles. This is a one-time capital investment.

  • **One-Time Unit Cost:** **$3,750**
  • *Impact:* Enables 180 youth to be trained annually for 5+ years.

See Digital Literacy Budget → Fund the Unit ($3,750)
