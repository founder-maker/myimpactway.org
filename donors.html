Healing Begins With Safety, Love, and Routine

We operate a 12-month Healing Farm program for children ages 4–10 focused on rescue, stabilization, and early learning.

Healing Farm: Year-1 Program Flow (Summary)

  • Phase 1 (Weeks 1–4): Intake, stabilization, medical care, predictable routines.
  • Phase 2 (Months 2–3): Trust-building, animal interaction, gardening, movement and music.
  • Phase 3 (Months 4–12): Emotional regulation, early learning, literacy, art, healthy responsibilities.
