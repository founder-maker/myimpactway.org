Operational Integrity: Our Commitment to Safeguarding

Our policy is simple: Every child deserves absolute safety, dignity, and privacy. Compliance and child protection guide every operational decision we make.

CORE MANDATES

  • **Zero Voluntourism:** We do not offer short-term, unskilled, or ad-hoc volunteer placements that involve interaction with children.
  • **Closed-Access Programs:** Public visits, donors, or non-essential personnel are strictly prohibited at the Healing Farm.
  • **Staff Vetting:** All staff and local partners undergo mandatory background checks and comprehensive child protection training.
  • **Clear Reporting Guidelines:** We maintain a non-retaliatory reporting structure for any suspected breach of policy.

Donor and Partner Assurance

We understand that our adherence to best practices is your assurance. We follow a strict governance structure with an independent board to maintain ethical and financial separation from all related commercial entities.
