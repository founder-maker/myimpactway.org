A Safeguarding-First Approach

The safety, privacy, and long-term well-being of the children we serve are our highest ethical priority. We are committed to global best practices in child protection.

Our Core Safeguarding Commitments

Image Policy (No Identification)

We strictly adhere to a policy of **not publishing identifiable images** of children in our residential care or digital literacy programs. All public photos are illustrative, stock, or heavily blurred to protect privacy.

Ethical Volunteer Screening

All on-site staff and volunteers undergo mandatory background checks, specialized trauma-informed care training, and sign a strict Code of Conduct before interacting with participants.

Trauma-Informed Care

Our program design, especially at the Healing Farm, is rooted in trauma-informed principles to avoid re-traumatization and support holistic recovery.
